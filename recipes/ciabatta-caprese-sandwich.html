Ciabatta Caprese Sandwich

Description

The ciabatta caprese sandwich is my favorite option for lunch. It's a quick lunch but it is absolutely delicious. This sandwich is a classic Italian vegetarian sandwhich and is the "perfect combination of fresh and vibrant flavors held together by delicious toasted ciabatta bread."

Ingredients

  • Ciabatta bun
  • Olive oil
  • Pesto
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Fresh mozzarella (I reccomend buying the mozzerella that is already sliced)
  • Fresh basil

Steps

  1. Step 1: Add 1 tablespoon olive oil to a cast iron pan over medium heat and a then add the ciabatta bread, interior side facing down.
  2. Step 2: Once the interior side of the bread has a golden color, remove the bread from the pan
  3. Step 3: Spread pesto sauce on the interior sides of the ciabatta buns (Make sure that the sides are dully covered with pesto)
  4. Step 4: Next, add the slices of mozzarella cheese to the bottom bun
  5. Step 5: Add a zigzag of balsamic vinegar on top of the mozzarella slices (don't put too much vinegar)
  6. Step 6: Add the tomato slices on top of the balsamic vinegar and mozzarella
  7. Step 7: Add the fresh basil leaves on top of the tomato slices
  8. Step 8: Lastly, put the top bun on top of the tomato slices to complete the sandwich.

Enjoy!
