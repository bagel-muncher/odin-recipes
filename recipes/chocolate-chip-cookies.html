Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Who doesn't like chocolate chip cookies? These cookies are a classic and are beloved by millions because they're soft, chewy, and absolutely irresistible.

This page contains the recipe to make amazing chocolate chip cookies. It might seem like the process is long and taxing, but baking these cookies are quite simple. Let's begin!

Ingredients

These are the kitchen staples you'll need to make the best chocolate chip cookies of your life:

  • 1 cup Butter:, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts

Steps

  1. Step 1: Gather your ingredients, making sure your butter is softened, and your eggs are room temperature.
  2. Step 2: Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  3. Step 3: Beat butter, white sugar, and brown sugar with an electric mixer in a large bowl until smooth.
  4. Step 4: Beat in eggs, one at a time, then stir in vanilla.
  5. Step 5: Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt.
  6. Step 6: Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and walnuts.
  7. Step 7: Drop spoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart onto ungreased baking sheets.
  8. Step 8: Bake in the preheated oven until edges are nicely browned, about 10 minutes.
  9. Step 9: Cool on the baking sheets briefly before removing to a wire rack to cool completely.
  10. Step 10: Store in an airtight container or serve immediately!

Enjoy!
